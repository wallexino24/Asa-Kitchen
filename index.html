Our Story

Cooking with memory. Serving with pride.

Áṣà Kitchen was born from a simple belief: that African food deserves the same reverence, presentation, and setting as any world cuisine. Our founder, Chef Tomi Adeyemi, grew up watching her grandmother cook over open fire in Ibadan — every meal a ritual, every spice a story.

Today, we bring that memory to the table with modern technique, seasonal sourcing, and an uncompromising respect for flavour. Every dish on our menu is a conversation between the old world and the new.
